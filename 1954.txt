Billboard Jan 2, 1954
Out in the Open

Publicist Max Rosey, formerly of the Bert Nevins office, New York 
is conducting a national college queens contest with plans for making 
it an annual event.

Rosey is dickering with Park, fair, theatre and dance hall people 
for franchise rights for local eliminations. Judging of the finals, 
January 8-10 will be held at the Hotel di Lido Miami will be by a panel 
consisting of Beth Meyerson, Yul Brynner, Robert Q. Lewis and Mrs. Arthur Murray
